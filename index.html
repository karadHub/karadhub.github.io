About Me

I'm all about building and optimizing modern cloud infrastructure. My focus is on making things more efficient and scalable by leveraging a few key technologies:

  • Kubernetes, Jenkins, and Terraform: I use these to deploy infrastructure, automate everything from the ground up, and drive robust CI/CD pipelines.
  • Multi-Cloud & DevSecOps: I'm passionate about integrating these strategies to build secure, reliable, and high-performing systems.

When I'm not hands-on with the tech, I'm sharing what I've learned whether it's mentoring a junior engineer, speaking at an event, or writing content.
